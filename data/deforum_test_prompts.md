## Camera Movement Options

Reference for the `camera` field:

| Camera | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| static wide shot with subtle zoom | establishing shots, confrontations |
| slow push-in on face | emotional close-ups, tension |
| handheld camera with slight shake | raw/anxious moments, action |
| steadicam following movement | following characters, transitions |
| low angle dolly forward | power dynamics, reveals |
| tilt up from ground level | reveals, scale |
| overhead crane shot descending | establishing, god's-eye view |
| pan across landscape | environment reveals |
| whip pan transition | scene transitions, energy |
| 360-degree orbit around subject | character focus, isolation |
| slow tracking shot following subject | contemplative movement |
| slow zoom out revealing context | reveals, pulling back |

## Style/Mood Tags

Combine these in the prompt:

- **Film style:** cinematic, noir, tech-noir, neo-noir, art film
- **Atmosphere:** mysterious, atmospheric, dark, dramatic, contemplative
- **Themes:** existential, philosophical, psychological, dystopian, surreal
- **Setting:** studio, cityscape, urban, night, minimalist
- **Story:** humanist, rebellion, confrontation
